My Dessert Philosophy

I believe that delicious cakes and sweets don't have to contain enormous amounts of sugar. They also don't have to contain any food coloring or inedible decorations to look pretty and appealing. All of my sweets are well balanced in flavor, without any nasty additions. I use fresh fruits and berries, I make my own fruit purees and nut pastes, I use the best quality ingredients and premium chocolates. With love and passion, I create cakes and desserts that will match your expectations perfectly.

Let's get acquainted!

Originally from Russia, I've lived in Tokyo for 6 years. Designer by education, painter (traditional and digital) by vocation, and a cook by love.

I'm married and recently gave birth to my second son. My brain needed to continue its creativity. But with a baby, brushes and canvases are out of reach. That's how I ended up in the world of desserts. I love to create, to cook, and to feed. My goal is to bring happiness, joy and celebration to people's lives with delicious and unique desserts. And also, I'd like to bring a taste of the most loved sweets from our home countries, that are very rare to find in Japan.
